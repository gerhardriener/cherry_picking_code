Instructions

In this experiment you can earn money depending on your decisions and the decisions of other participants. It is therefore important that you read the instructions carefully and completely.

Procedures

The experiment will last about 15 minutes. The payoffs are shown in ECU (Experimental Currency Units) where 1 ECU = 0.1 USD.

Details

Three participants interact with each other. They are called A, B1 and B2 . The role will be randomly assigned at the beginning of the experiment. It is very important that you familiarize with each role.
