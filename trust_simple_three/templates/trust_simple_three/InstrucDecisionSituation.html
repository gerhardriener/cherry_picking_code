Decision situation

Participant A can first choose between "Option L" and "Option R".

When choosing Option L the payments are 5 ECU for Participant A, 5 ECU for Participant B1 and 5 ECU for Participant B2.

If Option R is chosen by participant A, the payoffs are determined by B1 and B2. B1 and B2 can chose between two options:

  • "Option L" means for A a payment of 0 ECU and for the respective participant B 14 ECU.
  • "Option R" means for A a payment of 6 ECU and for the respective participant B 10 ECU.
Total payment for participant A is the sum of the decisions of participant B1 und B2.

The choice of player B1 and B2 are asked independetly of the choice of participant A. The decisions of the Bs are relevant when A chooses Option R, however you will always take a decision, assuming that Player A has chosen "Option R".

Participant A will not be informed about the decisions of participants B1 and B2. A only learns her own payoff at the end of the experiment.
